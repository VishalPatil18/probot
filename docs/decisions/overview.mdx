Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) capture the *why* behind ProBot's significant technical choices: the context, the options weighed, the decision, and its consequences. They're short, dated, and immutable - if a decision is later reversed, a new ADR supersedes the old one rather than editing it.

These records distil decisions originally captured in the project's build log (`claude/context.md`) and learning journal (`claude/learnings.md`).

## Format

Each record follows the same shape:

- **Context** - the forces at play and the problem to solve.
- **Decision** - what was chosen.
- **Alternatives considered** - what was rejected and why.
- **Consequences** - what this makes easy, what it makes harder, and the residual risk.
