ProBot is built in small, independently deployable stages. The Beta shipped the full end-to-end product; **Version 1.0** is the active line of work, refining UX, trust, SEO, performance, and a self-hostable bot runtime.

For the live, always-current status, see the [Roadmap page on the app](https://pro-bot.dev/roadmap) and the [Changelog](../changelog). The sections below summarise where things stand.

## Shipped today

The public product is fully functional:

- **Auth** - email/password plus Google and GitHub OAuth, magic-link sign-in, password reset, remember-me.
- **Bot Factory** - build a bot from PDF uploads and pasted text, with avatar, theme colour, personality, custom instructions, and suggested questions.
- **Knowledge & RAG** - PDF extraction, token-aware chunking, pgvector retrieval (top-K chunks injected into the system prompt), graceful full-context fallback.
- **BYO-key** - your Anthropic / OpenAI / Azure / Gemini key, held encrypted in your browser by default, or [envelope-encrypted on the server](/guides/managed-key-storage) if you opt in.
- **Public chat + embed** - a public `/u/<username>/chat` page and a one-line [embeddable widget](/embed-share).
- **Dashboard** - conversations, lead capture with email notifications, account settings, and a Terms-change banner.
- **Trust & GDPR** - [data export and account deletion](/guides/account-deletion) with a 7-day grace window.

## In progress (Version 1.0)

Marketing & trust pages, best-in-class SEO and docs, performance hardening (shared-state rate limiting, alerting), and a self-hosted bot-runtime architecture. Track the detail on the [live roadmap](https://pro-bot.dev/roadmap).
