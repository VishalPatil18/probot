Each bot has a **deployment mode**, decided at creation. It cannot be
switched later - the two modes have very different config surfaces.

## Managed (default, from Bot Factory)

The bot is served entirely by pro-bot.dev:

- Public chat at `pro-bot.dev/u/<username>/chat`
- One-line [embed widget](/embed-share)
- Nothing to install or maintain

This is the right choice for almost everyone. See [Managed hosting](/hosting/managed).

## Self-hosted (via npm package)

Install [`probot-self-hosted`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/probot-self-hosted)
in your own web app and render `<ProbotBot />`. Persona, knowledge,
provider, and theme all live in your code; your LLM key stays in your
backend. Optionally link the widget to the ProBot dashboard for
conversation and lead analytics.

Registering a self-hosted bot:

1. Open the sidebar bot switcher in the dashboard and click **Register
   self-hosted bot**.
2. Enter a name; we create the analytics entry and mint your first token
   (shown once).
3. `npm i probot-self-hosted` in your web app and render `<ProbotBot />`
   with the token in `dashboard.token`.

See [Self-hosting your bot](/self-hosted-bot/index) for the full walk-through.
