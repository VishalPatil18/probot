ProBot logs every conversation (anonymously) and captures recruiter details so you can follow up. All of it lives in your dashboard.

## Dashboard metrics

The dashboard home shows live tiles:

- **Total conversations** - with a live week-over-week change.
- **Total messages** - with a live week-over-week change.
- **Leads captured** - with a live month-over-month change.

The change figures are real (current period vs. the prior period), coloured green for up and red for down. A daily activity chart shows conversation volume over the last 7 days.

<Info>
  Conversations are logged anonymously; the only personal data captured is what
  a recruiter voluntarily submits in the lead form.
</Info>

## How leads are captured

After a few exchanges, an in-chat form invites the recruiter to leave their details:

- **Name** (required)
- **Email** (required)
- **Company** (required)
- **LinkedIn URL** (optional)

On submit, the lead is saved, the conversation is tagged with their email, and you get a dashboard notification (and an email, if you opted in).

## Managing leads

Open **Leads** for a bot to see captured details, jump to the originating conversation, and **export everything to CSV** (columns: captured_at, name, email, company, linkedin_url, bot_name, context_summary, conversation_id).

## Conversations

Open **Conversations** to browse anonymized chat transcripts per bot - useful for spotting common recruiter questions and improving your knowledge base.

## Retention

Conversations and leads are kept until you delete the bot or your account - there's no automatic time-based purge. Deleting your account removes everything after the 7-day grace window. See [Export & delete your account](/guides/account-deletion).
